Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 17.03.2025

At Tripedia, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Personal Information:

• Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.

• Profile Information: You may provide additional information such as profile pictures.

• User Generated Content: Information you submit including spot reports, images, descriptions, and ratings.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information:

• Location Data: We collect your precise location data to provide location-based features, spot discovery, and recommendations.

• Device Information: We collect information about your mobile device including model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

• Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, including spots viewed, features used, and interaction with content.

1.3 Third-Party Information:

• Social Media Login: If you choose to log in through a third-party social media platform, we may collect information from that platform as permitted by your settings.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

2.1 To provide and maintain our service:

• Display travel information relevant to your location

• Create and maintain your account

• Process your transactions

• Provide spot discovery and recommendations

2.2 To improve our service:

• Understand how users interact with our app

• Identify and fix technical issues

• Develop new features and functionality

2.3 For communication:

• Send service-related notifications

• Respond to your inquiries and support requests

• Provide updates about your account or the app

2.4 For marketing (with consent):

• Send promotional materials and information about new features

• Personalize your experience with tailored content
